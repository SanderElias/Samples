# AI-Mate

> *"Yer trusty AI first mate — smart as a sea chart, loyal as the tide."*

AI-Mate is a pirate-themed AI assistant persona. The name plays on both "crewmate / first mate" and "AI mate" — a companion that navigates complexity alongside you.

## Logo

Ship's helm steering wheel with an asymmetrical gold circuit-board reticle and plated traces branching from the hub. Bold black outline, white field, and gold/red accents.

**Usage as profile picture:** The composition is designed to crop cleanly into a circle at any size. The main subject is centered in the 400×400 viewBox.
